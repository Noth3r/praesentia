Praesentia

Fill your presence without hassle

Simple Python-based web application to allow UGM students to fill their QR presence list without having another device in hand.

Important! Using Praesentia will log you out from your SIMASTER mobile app. Please logout and re-login after using Praesentia.


Don't worry, your data will be safe. This app doesn't even have a database.
Still concerned? Just host Praesentia yourself. It's open source, see it on GitHub.
